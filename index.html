About Us

since 2010
charity life is a global non-profit organisation which was founded in the year 1919. Today, it is India's leading independent child rights NGO. As of December 2019, we work in 18 states of India. Started in 2008 in India, and registered as 'Bal Raksha Bharat', we have changed the lives of more than 10.1 million (1.1 crore) children till date. In the year 2019, we reached 12.64 lakh children. We are proud to be the world’s leading expert on children, delivering lasting results for millions of vulnerable girls and boys. By saving the world’s children, we transform their lives and the future we share.
